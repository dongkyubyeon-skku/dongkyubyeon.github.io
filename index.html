Painter born from
the sea of Busan

Born in 2000 in Busan, South Korea, I pursue Fine Art with a focus on painting at Sungkyunkwan University (SKKU). My work explores the tension between memory and presence — the way light falls differently on familiar surfaces, and how colour holds emotional weight beyond its visual form.
